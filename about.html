As a lifelong visual artist and practicing architectural designer, I am always blending my experiences of the two disciplines through my artwork, finding artistic and architectural inspirations from nature, travel, local architecture, and history. After studying architecture at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute for 5 years including a semester abroad in Rome, I returned to my hometown of Saratoga Springs and began channeling my studies into my artwork. Through watercolor, drawing, collage, and more, I am discovering and capturing architecture found in my community, history, travel, and my imagination.
